# Personal_Finance_Manager

A powerful, intuitive personal finance management application that helps you take control of your financial life. Track expenses, manage budgets, monitor investments, and achieve your financial goals with ease.

Features

Expense Tracking: Log and categorize daily expenses with ease

Budget Management: Create custom budgets and track spending against them

Multi-Account Support: Manage multiple bank accounts, credit cards, and cash accounts

Investment Portfolio Tracking: Monitor stocks, mutual funds, and other investments

Financial Reports: Generate insightful reports and visualizations of your spending patterns
